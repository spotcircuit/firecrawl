What types of insurance certificates can Hawk-I process? +

Hawk-I can process all standard ACORD forms, custom COI formats, international insurance certificates, and even handwritten documents. Our AI is trained on over 1 million certificate variations.

How accurate is the AI-powered validation? +

Hawk-I achieves 99.8% accuracy in data extraction and validation. The system continuously learns from corrections and validations, improving accuracy over time.

Can Hawk-I integrate with our existing systems? +

Yes, Hawk-I offers REST API integration, webhook support, and pre-built connectors for popular ERP, procurement, and risk management systems. Implementation typically takes 2-4 weeks.
